Greetings Lord Vader!

The Rebels are attempting to land on our base of operations in their fleet of Millenium Falcons. Your objective is to knock them out of the sky!

Input Controls
--------------
A or Left  - Swing left
D or Right - Swing right
W or Up    - Thrust forward
ESC        - Exit game


GUI Controls
------------
"Vader Helmet"   Button    - Play Darth Vader Audio Quote
"Color Crystal"  Dropdown  - Change color of saber
"Use the Force!" Button    - Apply random force to all Millenium Falcons on the screen (Uses Lua)
